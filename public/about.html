About Me

Hello!

My name is Xiaonan Shi, and I am passionate about combining my diverse background in environmental engineering and computer science to create impactful technological solutions. I hold a Ph.D. in Environmental Engineering, which I completed in 2022, and a Master's degree in Computer Science, earned in 2024. Academic Journey

My academic journey began with a deep commitment to addressing environmental challenges. During my Ph.D. program, I focused on Understanding the Interfacial Processes of Reactive Nanobubbles Toward Agricultural Applications. My research has led to significant advancements in sustainable agriculture, contributing to:

  • 15 Journal Publications: Recognized and cited by peers, with over 1100 total citations.
  • 2 Book Chapters: Contributions to comprehensive academic resources in environmental engineering.
  • 2 Patents: Innovations that have been commercialized, demonstrating the practical application of my research.

Realizing the transformative potential of technology, I pursued a Master's degree in Computer Science to enhance my technical skill set. Throughout my studies, I have developed a solid foundation in both front-end and back-end technologies, equipping me with the skills necessary to build robust, scalable web applications. My coursework and projects have provided me with hands-on experience in:

  • Front-End Development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Angular
  • Back-End Development: Node.js, Express, Python, Django, and Ruby on Rails
  • Database Management: SQL, MongoDB, and PostgreSQL
  • Version Control: Git and GitHub
  • Deployment: Docker, Kubernetes, and cloud services like AWS and Heroku

Professional Interests
